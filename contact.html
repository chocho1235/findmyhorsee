Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does FindMyHorse work?

FindMyHorse aggregates listings from multiple horse platforms worldwide, allowing you to search across all of them in one place. When you find a horse you like, we connect you directly to the original platform.

Is FindMyHorse really free to use?

Yes, FindMyHorse is completely free to use. We don't charge any fees, subscriptions, or commissions. Our service is funded through partnerships with the platforms we connect to.

How do I buy a horse through FindMyHorse?

FindMyHorse helps you discover horses from multiple platforms. When you find a horse you're interested in, we redirect you to the original listing platform where you can contact the seller and complete the purchase.

Can I sell my horse through FindMyHorse?

FindMyHorse is a search engine, not a selling platform. To sell your horse, you'll need to list it on one of the platforms we connect to, and it will automatically appear in our search results.

How often are listings updated?

We update our listings in real-time from the connected platforms. New horses are added as soon as they're listed on the original platforms, ensuring you always see the most current inventory.
