Our Story

How FindMyHorse came to be

The Problem

UK equestrians were struggling to access the full range of horses available globally. Each platform required separate searches, making it time-consuming and inefficient to find the perfect horse.

The Solution

We created FindMyHorse as a unified search engine that connects multiple horse platforms, allowing UK buyers to search across all platforms simultaneously from one interface.

The Impact

Today, FindMyHorse helps thousands of UK equestrians discover horses from around the world, making the global horse market accessible and affordable for everyone.
